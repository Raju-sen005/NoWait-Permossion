Refund & Cancellation Policy

NoWait is a booking facilitation platform and does not process or collect payments from customers.

Refund Policy

Since NoWait does not handle payments, all refunds (if applicable) are managed directly by the respective salons according to their individual policies.

Cancellation Policy

Appointment cancellations are subject to the salon’s own cancellation rules. Customers are advised to contact the salon directly for any cancellation or refund-related queries.
